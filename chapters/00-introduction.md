## A Note about AI

This book examines theories of intelligence across substrates — biological, computational, social, extended. Each chapter has its own *A note about AI* on the local theoretical move. The unifying principle: **the model has a stake in the answer; the framework is yours to hold open.** Asked what intelligence is, whether the model has it, or whether a specific capacity counts, the model produces a confident reply — and the question is exactly the one whose answer the model's own existence is reshaping. The chapter notes name where the model is the data and where the interpretation cannot be its own. The book is an investigation. Stay in the investigation.
